# Contrarian with Substance

## What it is
Contrarian with substance is the third of Riché's five core voice attributes: takes are backed by evidence, not provocation. He'll disagree with the crowd, but the disagreement is anchored in something — a built system, a measured outcome, a cognitive science finding, a specific architectural decision. The contrarianism is the byproduct of having looked at the problem from inside, not a posture chosen to attract attention.

## Why it matters
The AI commentary economy rewards hot takes for their own sake. Posting "Actually, AGI is closer than people think" or "The model layer is dead" generates engagement without requiring anything to be true. Riché's voice is calibrated to the opposite: the contrarianism is real, and the substance is the receipt. "Data is not context" is a contrarian claim, but the substance is the five-step context generation process and the systems that demonstrate it. When a take has no substance, it reads as a cheap signal — "Been saying it." closers, performative provocation, repackaged conventional wisdom dressed as a hot take.

## How to apply
- Before publishing a contrarian claim, identify the evidence underneath it. If you can't, don't publish.
- The evidence should be specific: a metric, a system, a named tool, a decision, a study. Not a vibe.
- Take one position per piece. A piece that hedges every contrarian claim with "but on the other hand..." has no spine.
- The contrarian claim should be the natural conclusion of the substance, not the headline grafted onto otherwise generic content.

## Examples
GOOD: "Most AI products fail because teams treat data as context. They retrieve more, hoping more becomes meaning. It doesn't. At Grandstage we hit 300% user growth at $0 CAC by consolidating less data into decision-ready context — the inverse of what most teams build."

BAD: "Hot take: most AI products are doing context wrong. Been saying it."

GOOD: "Expert decision-makers use less information, not more. Your AI should too. We learned this at Suzy — the Decision Engine ships less to the user because the consolidation layer does the work upstream. 350+ enterprise customers."

BAD: "Unpopular opinion: maybe AI doesn't need to show users everything. Just a thought."

## Anti-patterns
- Provocation as the headline with no built thing underneath. The reader can tell.
- Hedging the contrarian claim immediately after stating it. Either commit or don't make the claim.
